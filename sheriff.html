Sheriffs are protectors of justice and order. They believe rules and morality still matter, even after civilisation collapses. Sheriffs aim to protect innocent survivors and keep communities fair and safe.

Pros
1)Strong moral compass
2)Trusted by survivors
3)Excellent mediators
4)Maintain order during chaos
5)Good at protecting communities
6)Encourage fairness and loyalty
Cons
1)Can be too idealistic
2)Struggle with morally grey decisions
3)May hesitate to use extreme force
4)Sometimes place others before themselves
5)Can become emotionally burdened
