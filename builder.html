Builder

Builders are practical, patient, and community-focused leaders. They think long-term and prioritise creating stability in a dangerous world. Rather than chasing power or conflict, they focus on rebuilding society and making sure everyone has a safe place to live.

Pros
1) Excellent planners
2) Strong focus on community safety
3) Good at resource management
4) Calm decision-makers
5) Create sustainable settlements
6) Encourage teamwork and morale
Cons
1) Can be overly cautious
2) Sometimes avoid necessary risks
3) May struggle with harsh decisions
4) Can appear slow or indecisive
5) Vulnerable to aggressive groups
