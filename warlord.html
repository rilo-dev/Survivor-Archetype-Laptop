Warlords are aggressive, fearless, and power- driven leaders. They believe strength is the only true law in the apocalypse. Warlords prioritise survival at any cost and are willing to make brutal decisions to protect their people.

Pros

2)Quick decision-makers
3)Effective fighters
4)Fearless in dangerous situations
5)Excellent at defending territory
Cons

2)May rely too heavily on violence
